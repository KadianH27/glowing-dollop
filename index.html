About Me

Hello! My name is Kadian Hall, and I am a rising high school senior with a passion for blending the wonders of nature with the advancements of technology. I aspire to major in Computer Engineering, focusing on integrating technology into the fields of Biology and Chemistry. From a young age, I've been fascinated by the natural world and its potential, which has led to my ultimate goal of innovating in agricultural technology, creating solutions that enhance sustainable farming practices, and improving access to natural health remedies. My interests include reading, gardening, and trying new food recipes.
